Welcome ! The idea leading to this website started from 2 facts : Many people doesn't do anything to help the ones suffering mainly because of a lack of knowledge, and the best way to learn is having fun. Our objective was then easy to find, sensitizing people to humanitarian causes in a playful way.

This website allows you to answer an infinite quizz about various elements representing the level of wellness of people and countries. The questions are generated by communicating with an handmade Open Data API (http://54.154.27.229:9876/opendata/question - ) which uses ontologies to deduce facts on specified subjects.

And you are not alone ! Thanks to a gamification system, each good answer give you points (that you can find in the top left of the page), letting you compete and compare your knowledge with your friends ! For that, some scripts (/static/js/*) manage a player, with points, levels, and achievement (not implemented yet).
